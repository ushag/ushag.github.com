After traveling along most of the east and west coasts of the United States, including Alaska, Hawaii and Puerto Rico, not to mention the 15 National Parks, we’ve been finding it difficult of late trying to place some of the memories.

Was that short diversion we took in the lush green at Smokies or the Rockies? The long walk along a gurgling river, with not another soul in sight for hours, was in Alaska or the Acadia?

This year, I am planning on taking a break from real travel (atleast in the short term..heh) and relive the fond memories from the wonderful natural sights at the many parks and take a meandering (photo-aided) stroll through the favortite places once again.

So hang on tight and ramble along on a fun journey..
